We believe that Holocaust education should be taught in an age-appropriate way in grade 6 in every province and territory in Canada. If you agree, please fill out this form to help us convince Education Ministers.

Liberation75 was founded by Marilyn Sinclair in 2018. Marilyn is a daughter of Holocaust survivors. This not-for-profit organization is dedicated to teaching the lessons of the Holocaust through innovation and collaboration with Holocaust organizations from around the world. Holocaust education teaches students to protect freedom and democracy and be better citizens.
